Stuffing

Description

Ok I'm pretty sure the only time people ever eat stuffing is during Thanksgiving. It's kinda gross, and I don't even know what it is really.

Ingredients

  • 30 slices lightly toasted white bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large finely chopped onion
  • 2 stalks finely chopped celery
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 large lightly beaten eggs
  • 2 teaspoons rubbed sage
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (as needed)

Directions

  1. Spread toasted breadslices out and let sit for 24 hours to dry
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F, lightly grease pan
  3. Crush bread slices into crumbs with a rolling pin, and place in bowl
  4. Saute butter and vegetables in pan till soft
  5. Add chicken broth and eggs to bread crumbs, then add vegetables
  6. Bake until brown and crisp. Serve
